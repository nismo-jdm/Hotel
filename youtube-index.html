About Vortex

Vortex is a next-generation YouTube downloader built with speed, privacy, and simplicity as core principles. We believe downloading content you have the right to download should be fast, beautiful, and effortless.

Founded by a team of engineers passionate about the open web, Vortex processes millions of download requests with zero data retention. We don't track you, we don't store your links, and we don't sell your data.

OUR MISSION

To provide the world's fastest, cleanest video download experience — for everyone, everywhere, always free.

TECHNOLOGY

Vortex is built on a distributed edge-computing infrastructure, ensuring sub-second response times regardless of your location. Our adaptive quality engine automatically selects the best available format for your connection and device.

Vortex is intended for downloading content you own or have permission to download — such as your own videos, Creative Commons content, or videos for offline personal use in accordance with YouTube's Terms of Service.
